DIRECTIONS

Directions from Cork/Killarney via N22.

As you approach Tralee turn right at new Roundabout, straight thru Ballingarry roundabout heading for Listowel, drive 2 miles. Next roundabout exit left fr Tralee Tech Park Next roundabout turn right - this is the Listowel road. Drive 1/4 mile you will see couple of warehouses on your right. At Boyle Patio & stove centre turn right, Alderwood Road. Drive 1 mile, entrance on left drive up through trees.

Directions from Limerick via N21

Come through Abbeyfeale, before Castleisland take exit for Tralee, right again at the roundabout for Tralee, drive 7 miles then approaching Tralee, with Ballingarry Hotel on your left, take 3rd exit for Listowel. Drive 2 miles. Next roundabout exit left fr Tralee Tech Park Next roundabout turn right - this is the Listowel road. Drive 1/4 mile you will see couple of warehouses on your right. Boyle Patio & stove center turn right, Alderwood Road. Drive 1 mile, entrance on left drive up thru trees.
