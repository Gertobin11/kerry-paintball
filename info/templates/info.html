Important Info before playing

What should you wear?

Come wearing old boots, old clothes and strong shoes, even a pair of football boots can be used as the ground can be slippery at times. A beanie hat is also recommended. We suggest bringing a fresh change of clothes, especially socks as it can get quiet wet at times and also a spare bag for you mucky boots.All the safety equipment is provided by us.

Do we need to pay in advance?

A deposit is required to book guns, as we only have so many guns we do not want to disapoint other players unecessarily if someone is a no show. Deposits will be refunded once the group shows up. Deposits are non refundable but can be used again with 3 weeks notice given. To pay a deposit simply use revolut to @annaodpti

Is there a card facility on site?

Unfortunately as we are in the middle of the woods it is not a possibility at the moment, so we advise our players to have cash before they come to the site.
